About Flavour

Flavour is a vertically integrated bespoke high-tech knitting company that creates everyday clothing, smart formal or casual, depending on the client's wants.

Our Products

  • Custom-knitted sweaters
  • High-tech knitted dresses
  • Smart casual knitted wear

Our Vision

At Flavour, we believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality, personalized clothing that makes them look and feel their best. We envision a future where fast fashion is replaced by sustainable, made-to-order garments that are designed to last a lifetime.

Meet Our Founder

Michael Kir

Michael Kir is the founder and CEO of Flavour. With a background in textile manufacturing and a keen interest in technology, Michael set out to create a company that would merge cutting-edge knitting techniques with personalized design.
